Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Step 1: Slice the potatoes and place them in cold water to prevent browning.
  2. Step 2: Sift the flour and seasonings, then gradually stir in water to make a batter.
  3. Step 3: Drain and dry the sliced potatoes, then dip in the batter.
  4. Step 4: Fry the potatoes until golden brown and crispy.
